**We do not collect any personal data.**

The App:

- Does not collect personal information
- Does not track your location

- Does not contain advertisements

All game data (settings, preferences) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
