How to Play

Trash Talk Solitaire is classic Klondike solitaire with a snarky British commentator.

  • Goal: Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles (top right), sorted by suit from Ace to King.
  • Tap the stock pile (top left) to draw cards.
  • Drag cards between tableau piles, building down in alternating colors.
  • Double-tap a card to auto-move it to the foundation if valid.
  • Kings can be moved to empty tableau columns.

Settings

  • Mute: Toggle commentary voice on/off
  • Speech Rate: Adjust how fast the commentator speaks
